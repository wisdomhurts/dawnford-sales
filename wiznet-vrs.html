HOW IT WORKS

Real-time kinematic corrections across BC.

With WizNet VRS, users access RTK correction data from a network of reference stations, allowing for highly accurate surveying and mapping. This unique network offers wide coverage and fast signal acquisition using NTRIP technology.

COVERAGE AREAS

Vancouver Island, Sunshine Coast, Sea to Sky corridor, Greater Vancouver, Fraser Valley, and Merritt. Expanding to BC interior — Kamloops to Osoyoos.
